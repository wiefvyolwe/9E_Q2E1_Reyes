Noli me Tangere






Characters in Noli me Tangere and their description
Maria Clara
  • Maria Clara, whose full name is María Clara de los Santos, is the daughter of Capitán Tiago and Doña Pía Alba.
  • She is a woman admired and looked up to by other women for her achievements.
  • She is the fiancee of Crisostomo Ibarra
Kapitan Tiago
  • His full name is Don Santiago de los Santos and he is the husband of Doña Pía Alba
  • He is an influential and rich business man in San Diego
  • He is a short, fat man with clear skin but he is a respected member in their society
Crisostomo Ibarra
  • Crisostomo Ibarra, whose full name is Juan Crisóstomo Ibarra y Magsalin, is a wealthy and young student
  • He studied in Europe for 7 years but came back to the Philippines after his father's death
  • He is the main protaginist in the novel
Padre Damaso
  • His full name is Dámaso Verdolagas
  • He is a Spanish Priest living in the Philippines who has a lot of power which he uses against the people living there
  • He is a mean and self-important person who is very opinionated and acted very superior to other people
Tiya Isabel
  • Her full name is Isabel de los Santos who is also the sister of Kapitan Tiago
  • Doña Pía Alba died giving birth to Maria Clara so she offered help
  • She helped Kapitan Tiago take care of Maria Clara and was like a parent figure to Maria Clara
Doña Pía Alba
  • She is the mother of Maria Clara and the wife of Kapitan Tiago
  • She is a woman of wealth and is a very well known woman in their society
  • She died right after giving childbirth to Maria Clara
